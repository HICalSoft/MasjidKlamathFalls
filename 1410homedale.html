Masjid Klamath Falls - 1410 Homedale Rd. Address Directions

Prayer Space Address:
1410 Homedale Rd. Klamath Falls OR 97603

Google Maps Link: https://maps.app.goo.gl/yxMCrA9qEWEkHPYG9

Directions

Note: It can take a few minutes to park and figure out how to get to the prayer space when it's your first time coming here. Parking is tricky, this is a residence, not a business. The easiest parking is across the street on the shoulder, then you will cross the road. Otherwise, parking on the driveway roundabout is first-come-first-serve and can be tricky to maneuver around.

1. Park your car as close as possible to the 1410 Homedale Rd. address, either in the driveway, or across the street on the shoulder of the road. Head to the main house.

2. Walk on the wooden pathway towards the front door.

3. Enter the front door. The bathroom, if you need to make wudu, is the first door on your left. The prayer space is the 2nd left.

4. Enter the prayer space for Jumuah prayer (the second left). Leave your shoes on the tile.
